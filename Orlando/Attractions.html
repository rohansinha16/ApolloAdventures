Disney World

Disney world hosts one of the most attractive spots to relax with family and friends as well as is home to thrilling rides and exciting shopping areas. Disney world boasts many seemingly magical lands that will dazzle children and parents alike. Originally built to supplement the original Disney Land, Disney world began to grow in size and added many of it’s own attractions, which makes it one of the most visited and well known places in Orlando. When visitors think of Orlando, Disney World is almost always the second thing they think of.

Seaworld

Being the tenth most visited amusement park, Seaworld is a huge park that consists of many “seas” that make up the different regions of the park. Including places for childrens, adults, and both alike, tourists can taste the sea both physically and visually. Each sea has a different theme and centers all attractions at the respected sea around the theme, resulting in “seas” that seem like wonderlands to visitors. Seaworld is both enjoyable and a must visit place for tourists visiting Orlando.
