Itinerary

So What's the Plan?

Sunday: Get on flight and find hotel room. You will be riding on Delta Airlines from 7:50 am to 11:58 am (3 hr and 8 min flight w/ 1 hour time difference)= $372 round trip per person. Resort with 229 US dollars per night * 5 nights = 1145 dollars. 3-4 person room.

Monday: Be up by 6:30 for your bus ride over to one of the most attractive spots to relax with family and friends as well as being home to thrilling rides and exciting shopping areas; Disney World! Stay as long as you want but you wonâ€™t want to miss the light show on the water at the end of the night. Pricing varies.

Tuesday: Visit the tenth most visited amusement park in the United States; Seaworld. Spend the day seeing the largest, smallest and coolest looking animals of the ocean. The day begins at 9 am so feel free to sleep in. Have a wonderful lunch with the famous Shamu and come back to the hotel around 3 to spend the rest of the day resting and relaxing. Tomorrow won't be as relaxing.

Wednesday: A busy day today. Wake up around 7 and take a ride over to the Legoland Florida Resort. Perfect for children 2-12 or even those young at heart. Stay, shop, go on some rides, spend the half day however you please but be sure to make it back to the hotel by 1:30 for your bus ride to Typhoon Lagoon. Another amazing water park for you and your family to have fun. Dont miss out on the giant wave pool and surfing programs!

Thursday: The last day on your trip will definitely be a blast just like the rest. Spend the day at the world renowned Universal Studios. Probably the best amusement park out of all the ones you've gone to. Definitely an all day experience as well. Bus leaves for the park at 7:30 so wake up, get breakfast and be on your way at about 6 am. Once you get to Universal studios make sure to make it to all the fantastic rides. By going to Universal Studios today you have saved the best for last. Make it home for a dinner in your hotel and pack for your flight tomorrow. $136 for one day, gets cheaper per day the longer you stay.

Friday: Leave Orlando for a 7:40 am Delta Airline flight to Minneapolis which is scheduled to arrive back home at 10:04 am.
