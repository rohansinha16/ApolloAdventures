Shimbashi Nightlife

Areas like shimbashi offer many choices and things to do at night. Mainly occupied by Japanese salarymen after work, these places host a very comfortable environment and have many small shops. Tourists may not only enjoy the nightlife but also enjoy fine food and cheap souvenirs from the local street vendors.

Akihabara

This area in japan is considered an otaku paradise. Bustling with excitement and activity, shops line the streets with products ranging from machine parts to anime seasons and electronics. Tourists may experience japanese culture through their technology and cartoons in an environment much like a busy plaza.

Sushi!
