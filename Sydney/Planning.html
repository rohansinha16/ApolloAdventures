Itinerary

So What's the Plan?

Sunday - Monday: Hop on a plane from 2:52 - 4:51 pm to Los Angeles. Then from Los Angeles take the 9:50 pm flight to fly over to Australia the next morning at 7:50 am. You will be staying at the Harbour Rocks Hotel Sydney with $191 per night.

Tuesday: Today you will be visiting the all famous Sydney Opera House of Australia. Here you can explore the unique architecture and platform while also learning about its construction as you listen and watch an art performance.

Wednesday: Today you will be taking a day of challenge as you go through an actual simulation of puzzle games as you try to escape a room by your brain. This will cost about $20-30 dollars per person.

Thursday: Today take a detour of the Sydney Harbour Bridge and then go and catch a bite to eat at the The Rocks with its famous special cafe’s, pubs, and tea rooms.

Friday: Enjoy the Pacific seas as you try to learn how to surf with Australian Surf Tours with one on one lessons being $60 for hour and $100 for two.

Saturday: This day’s activity is one truly worthy of being listed as an itinerary for Apollo Adventures. Today you will be leading an adventure at the Eagle ROck Adventures where you will do activities such as abseiling, canyoning, hiking, etc. Canyoning prices anywhere from $220-260 per person with mutliple canyons to choose from. Abeseiling costs approximately $160-200. Contact Eagle Adventures for details at 1-300-957-919.

Sunday: Catch the 1:15 am plane to LA, California at 8:00 am the next day. Then ride the 7:05 pm to 12:37 am flight back to Minneapolis.
