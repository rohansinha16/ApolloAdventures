Mission Room Escape

Mission Room Escape Sydney is an actual simulation of puzzle games(escape the room/room escape) where the objective is to escape the room by your brain. We make it more than just a game but whole new unique experience by adopting the latest technology and give you endless surprises. Some of the challenges require teamwork, so you and your friends will need to work together. Mission Real Life Room Escape is a fun and challenging bonding experience for friends and family.

Eagle Rock Adventures

Eagle Rock Adventures is a leading adventure company based in Sydney Australia. Activities include abseiling, canyoning, hiking, climbing and kayaking. During winter the company runs daily Blue Mountains abseiling adventures with Sydney CBD pick-up included. Exhilarating jumps and challenging but rewarding abseil down the waterfalls. The location is picturesque and the staff are fantastic.
