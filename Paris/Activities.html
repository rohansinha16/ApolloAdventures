Wine Tasting

Wine tasting in Paris is the ultimate experience for people interested in doing a little wine tourism right in the heart of Paris. You will learn everything there is to know about French wines from the differences in their taste to the regions they originate from. Tourists will also learn how to properly order a bottle in the restaurant or a wine bar. The experience is interesting, enjoyable, and easy for anyone to learn. The wine seminars are targeted towards beginners who simply love wine.

Skydiving

Skydiving is a high adrenaline sport activity which consists of jumping from a height ranging from hundreds of meters to several thousand meters with a parachute. There are skydiving teams and drop zones located right outside Paris. Visitors can discover the thrill of skydiving near Paris giving tourists the perfect view of both the countryside and city. Sightseers can choose between style and accuracy skydiving, free flying, vertical formation skydiving, and wind tunnel skydiving.

Culinary Tours

France is famous internationally for its agriculture and long creative power base for delicious recipes. French cuisines are famous for the rich tastes and subtle nuances with long and rich history. A meal is consisted of three courses including the introductory/soup course, the main course, and the cheese/dessert course. Some of the cuisines include foie gras, pot au feu, and macarons. When taking a culinary tour at Paris, tourists can at the same time eat delicious authentic foods while also exploring Paris' best neighborhoods thus giving a brief history of the area. This kind of tour simply will not disappoint.
