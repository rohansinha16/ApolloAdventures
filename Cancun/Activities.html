Dolphinaris Cancun

One of the most exciting activities to do in Cancun, Mexico is swimming with the dolphins. This interactive dolphin program provides an up-close experience with dolphin tricks and a chance to take a dorsal fin and belly ride around the tank. Dolphinaris Cancun is the most exciting, comfortable and safe interactive dolphin encounter with experienced trainers who will even explain dolphin behavior and biology; an amazing activity to do with your family or friends.

Senor Frog’s Cancun

Senor Frog’s provides the night of your life as you drink and dance to live music and karaoke while dining on international cuisine. Located in the heart of Cancun’s nightspots there is an endless amount of fun awaiting you. This extremely enjoyable evening includes a domestic open bar after 9 PM and at the end of the party you will walk home with a free souvenir yard-length beer glass. Do not miss out on this extraordinary opportunity.

Open Water Diver Training

Cancun provides travelers with the experience of a lifetime; 3 days of unparalleled amazement as you earn your own PADI certification. Expertly instructed and given top-of-the-line equipment to make your experience the best possible. Throughout the 3 days you will have brief classroom sessions and multiple pool and open-water-reef dives, covering the fundamentals to earn your lifetime diving license. An experience you will never forget.
